Creator

$0 /month

DōMs doors are open to all people, we highly recommend creating a Creator account to gain access to certain DōM resources for free and to help to maintain DōM

What's Included:

  • Access to clubs and gatherings
  • Access to DōM network and website
  • Access to space within Open Hours
POPULAR

Contributor

$10 /month

Wanting to be more active in DōM? Becoming a Contributor allows you greater access to DōM services, discounts on DōM related events, and a more established presence within the DōM community.

Everything in Creator, plus:

  • Active roles in clubs and gatherings
  • Discounts/waived fees on tickets and planning events
  • Limited access to DōM equipment
  • Access to space outside of Open Hours (per resident approval)
  • Showcase on website and base to promote projects
  • Ability to post missions on mission board

Catalist

$35 /month

For those who are willing to catalize DōM, not just financially, but through services and projects, your voice will be recognized and heard as we help to build this community together. Becoming a Catalist gives you greater accessibility to the community and its resources with fewer hoops to jump through.

Everything in Contributor, plus:

  • Active voice within the collective/community
  • Generous access to DōM equipment
  • Door code + access to space outside of Open Hours (within reason)
  • Ability to host events or clubs as DōM sponsored
  • Showcase and space/ability to sell items through website to promote projects
  • Priority mission board posting

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your membership at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my tier?

Absolutely! You can change your membership tier at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards through Stripe's secure payment processing.

Is there a trial period?

Start with a free Creator account to explore DōM. When you're ready to become more involved, upgrade to Contributor or Catalist!

About DōM

A collective space for creatives to breathe, be, and collaborate.

DōM is a community-driven creative collective built on the belief that collaboration fuels innovation. We provide a space — both physical and digital — where artists, designers, musicians, writers, and makers of all kinds can connect, share resources, and bring ideas to life together.
